## Senior Staff Machine Learning Engineer <span style="float: right; font-size: 0.8em; font-style: italic;">Nov 2022 - Present</span>
### Kindred, Part of Ocado Group

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of ML research and live production robotics. I've worked on real-time detection systems for deployed robotic units, continual learning pipelines that keep models from drifting as production conditions change, and statistical modeling to characterize how new hardware handles unfamiliar items.

On the research side, I've been exploring human-in-the-loop reinforcement learning to improve imitation learning models — diffusion policy and ACT — for robotic manipulation. I also built an LLM-powered tool for browsing academic papers, aimed at making conference literature more navigable is a side goal of becoming familiar with agentic workflows.

## Staff Machine Learning Engineer <span style="float: right; font-size: 0.8em; font-style: italic;">Sep 2017 - Oct 2022</span>
### Kindred, Part of Ocado Group

I built the company's first ML components for robotic grasping — combining segmentation, multi-outcome prediction, and classical vision methods to reach grasp success rates above 95% in production. The constant thread was closing the gap between research-grade models and systems that hold up under real operating conditions.
